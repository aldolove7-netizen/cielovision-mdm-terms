Device & Account Terms of Use

Terms for Accessing CieloVision Systems and Data

This page is presented during device enrollment and account sign-in for users accessing CieloVision-managed systems, including Microsoft 365 and internal applications.

Company-Owned Devices Only. Personal devices are not permitted to access CieloVision systems.

1. Device Ownership & Enrollment

  • Only CieloVision-owned and issued equipment may be used to access company systems.
  • All devices must be enrolled in CieloVision’s Mobile Device Management (MDM) platform.
  • Unmanaged or personal devices will be blocked automatically.

2. Security Controls

  • Security policies such as disk encryption, password enforcement, screen lock, and OS compliance are mandatory.
  • Devices not meeting security requirements may lose access until remediated.

3. Data Protection & Monitoring

  • Company data stored or accessed on devices remains the property of CieloVision.
  • Access, authentication, and device posture may be logged for security and compliance purposes.
  • If a device is lost, stolen, compromised, or decommissioned, company data may be removed.

4. User Responsibilities

  • Users must safeguard credentials and comply with all security procedures.
  • Lost or stolen devices must be reported immediately.
  • Unauthorized use of systems or devices may result in access revocation.

5. Support

For assistance, contact support@cielovision.com.
